The Python Language Summit 2025

<p><span style="font-size: 14.6667px;">The Python Language Summit 2025 occurred on May 14th in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania. Core developers and special guests from around the world gathered in one room for an entire day of presentations and discussions about the future of the Python programming language.</span></p><p><span style="font-size: 14.6667px;">The summit was organized by Emily Morehouse and Łukasz Langa, and blog posts were written by Seth Larson.
